The Story
As I walked through the MoCA museum in Shanghhai, I found myself in awe by the art pieces of street artists: L'Atlas, JonOne, and Tanc. Their small to large scale artworks made me realize how distant I've grown from
tangible art.
Being a digital designer has left my head buried in front of the computer screen - creating
digital art
that could be seen but not felt. All that I knew now was the
world of code.
Through this project, I wanted
to bridge
the gap between the physical and digital art world. To use my skills in both mediums to create an experience users can understand and
explore
simultanesouly.
CyberArt is a space where users have the opportunity
to blend
inspired art styles together through digital and physical means. Color palettes were chosen to
reflect
physical objects such as fruits, while music was chosen to create a studio-like ambience.

By painting and coding each piece personally, I've had my own first hand
experience
in bringing together two mediums into one.
I want to provide the same experience to all through CyberArt.
